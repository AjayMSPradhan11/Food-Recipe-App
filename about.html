About Us

Welcome to ReciFree

ReciFree celebrates the vibrant heritage of Nepali cuisine, particularly iconic Newar dishes like bara and chatamari, showcasing its rich cultural roots. Focused on healthy and sustainable cooking, the platform emphasizes fresh, seasonal, and locally-sourced ingredients inspired by traditional Nepali food systems. With engaging features like an interactive search bar, recipe sharing, and visual guides, ReciFree makes it easy for users to discover and master authentic recipes. By introducing global audiences to Nepal’s unique flavors and encouraging Nepalese abroad to reconnect with their roots, ReciFree also promotes culinary tourism and elevates Nepal’s hidden gastronomic gems.

ReciFree invites you to explore the delicious world of Nepali cuisine, whether you’re looking to recreate traditional dishes, discover new favorites, or simply learn more about the country’s rich culinary history. Let’s cook, share, and celebrate Nepali food together!
